Scribbles (Pencil Sketching)




Art is the desire of a man to express himself, to record the reactions of his personality to the world he lives in. This is the chance to make yourself express the best



Rules

  • Rule1 : The size of the paper shall be A3.
  • Rule2 : Time allotted is 60 minutes.
  • Rule3 : The topic will be given on the spot.
  • Rule4 : Materials should be arranged by the participants themselves.
  • Rule5 : Decision of the judge will be final and binding.
